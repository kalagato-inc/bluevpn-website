# How to Add Hyperlinks in Your Blog Posts

Your blog now supports **internal** and **external** hyperlinks for better SEO and user experience!

## How to Add Links in Sanity Studio

1. **Open Sanity Studio** at http://localhost:3333

2. **Open or create a blog post**

3. **Select the text** you want to turn into a link

4. **Click the link icon** (🔗) in the toolbar

5. **Configure the link**:

   ### For External Links (to other websites):
   - **Link Type**: Select "External Link"
   - **URL**: Enter full URL like `https://www.google.com`
   - **Open in new tab**: ✅ Check this (recommended for external links)

   ### For Internal Links (to your own pages):
   - **Link Type**: Select "Internal Link (to your site)"
   - **URL**: Enter path like `/streaming` or `/blog` or `/remote-work`
   - **Open in new tab**: ❌ Uncheck this (users stay on your site)

6. **Click "Apply"** and **Publish** the post

## Visual Differences

- **External links** will show a small arrow (↗) after the text
- **Internal links** will be slightly bolder
- All links have:
  - Blue color
  - Subtle underline
  - Hover effects

## SEO Benefits

### Internal Links (Best for SEO):
```
Check out our [streaming guide](/streaming) for more details.
Learn how to [secure your remote work](/remote-work).
```

**Benefits**:
- Helps Google understand your site structure
- Keeps users on your site longer
- Distributes "link juice" across your pages
- Improves crawlability

### External Links:
```
According to [Google's research](https://research.google.com)...
Read more on [Wikipedia](https://wikipedia.org)...
```

**Benefits**:
- Shows you did research
- Builds authority
- Provides value to readers

## Example Blog Post with Links

```
Remote work is here to stay, but it comes with security challenges.

Learn more about [VPN security best practices](/blog/vpn-security-tips)
on our blog.

According to [Cybersecurity Ventures](https://cybersecurityventures.com),
cybercrime damages will cost $10.5 trillion by 2025.

That's why you need a VPN. Check out our [gaming VPN features](/gaming)
or [streaming capabilities](/streaming).
```

## Tips for Good SEO:

1. **Use descriptive anchor text**:
   - ❌ Bad: "Click here"
   - ✅ Good: "VPN security best practices"

2. **Link to relevant internal pages** (2-3 per post)

3. **Add 1-2 authoritative external sources**

4. **Don't over-link** (makes content spammy)

5. **Use keywords in anchor text naturally**

## Current Internal Pages You Can Link To:

- `/` - Homepage
- `/streaming` - Streaming VPN
- `/gaming` - Gaming VPN
- `/travel` - Travel VPN
- `/remote-work` - Remote Work VPN
- `/blog` - Blog listing
- `/blog/[slug]` - Specific blog posts
- `/terms` - Terms & Privacy
